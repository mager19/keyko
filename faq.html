BATTERY FACTS

  • Did you know that the first ever battery was created by Alessandro Volta in 1798? Professor Volta was an Italian master in physics and chemistry and was praised and honored by Napoleon himself for the dedication of his work.
  • There is no battery in the world that lasts forever. All batteries have a lifespan. However, at KEYKO US, we provide longer lasting premium batteries, and some are rechargeable. For example, alkaline batteries are easily recharge.
  • Have you ever wondered how those rechargeable batteries exactly work? It starts off by reversing the chemical reaction. The chemical reaction that spurs batteries to life occurs when electrons flow from the negative to positive end of the battery. When recharging a battery, the process is reversed in chemical reaction by utilizing regular electric energy. 
  • Did you know that you can extend the lifespan of many types of alkaline batteries by storing them at low temperatures, such as in the freezer of your refrigerator? The low temperature slows the various chemical reactions in the battery, allowing it to last much longer than the average expectancy. 
  • Did you know that car catteries are the number one reason for road service calls in the United States? As the weather and outside temperatures decrease, car battery efficiency deceases along with it!.
  • We are here to dispel a popular myth—a battery does not discharge faster when placed on a concrete floor. 
  • Here is a popular fun fact you can impress your friends with—A car battery contains 18 pounds of lead and over 1 pound of sulphuric acid.
  • Consumers in the United States purchase over 3 billion batteries every year to power a variety of products such as radios, toys, watches, laptops, power tools, phones and so much more!
